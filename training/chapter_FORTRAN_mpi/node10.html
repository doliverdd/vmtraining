Collective Communications

Until this point we've only discussed communications coming from a single process and going to a single process. MPI also contains collective communications which loosely defined are those communications that involve multiple processors on the sending end, the receiving end or both. The MPI_Barrier command that was described in the last section, is often included as a collective communication. In the following sections we will describe some of the most commonly used communications of this type. The reader should be aware that this is a rich, complex and useful area within MPI, and these notes barely scratch the surface.
