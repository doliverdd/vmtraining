The FORALL Statement

FORALL is introduced as an alternative to the DO-loop. The contents of a FORALL statement can be executed in any order. FORALL gives identical results whether applied sequentially or in parallel. Using FORALL is especially useful where assignments are based on array indices. 
    FORALL (I=1:1000,J=1:1000) A(I,J)=I+J
We can also use FORALL in the following form: 
    FORALL (I=1:1000, J=1:1000)
    A(I,J)=I+J
    B(I,J)=A(I,J)
    END FORALL
