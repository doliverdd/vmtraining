Introduction to High Performance Computing

Welcome to the University of South Florida High Performance Computing Boot Camp.The purpose of this seminar is to give students and faculty of USF a working knowledge of the High Performance Computing resources available at USF. During this week you will be introduced to some new and hopefully exciting computational tools. While we will be covering some programming topics, this not a class on programming. It would be impossible to teach this course without touching on some Computer Science topics, but we will be covering these areas only to enhance your understanding. Your time will be divided into discussion followed by practical practice examples. Questions are encouraged at any time.
